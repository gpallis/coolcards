The Coolest Cards of Magic: The Gathering

What's this site about?

This site was created to as a way to celebrate - and rate - the very coolest cards that Magic: The Gathering has to offer. It's open to anyone who plays Magic, from kitchen-table newbies to PTQ grinders and everyone in between.

What do you mean by cool?

Whatever you want it to mean! Some might cherish the sheer power of Jace or Jitte, while to other players a versatile engine like Birthing Pod is far more interesting. Still others might be moved by a tremendous flavour win such as Army of Damned, a unique oddity like Dryad Arbor, or the the awesome impact of dropping a Craterhoof Behemoth onto the table. We'd like to give all of these cards a chance to strut their stuff, outside of the rigid selectivity of the competitive metagame.

Why haven't you included my favourite card?

Efforts have been made to include awesome cards for every sort of player, but there's no accounting for taste! Some deliberate choices, however, were to restrict the card pool to the Modern era, and to exclude cards such as Lightning Bolt whose primary appeal lies entirely with their raw power relative to their cost; there are plenty of other sites on which such cards can win their plaudits, along with the whole field of competitive magic.

No, but seriously, you should include my favourite card.

Maybe you're right! Feel free to email suggestions to me here.

What next?

I'll keep updating the site with awesome cards from future sets as they get printed. For now, check out the leaderboard or get voting.
